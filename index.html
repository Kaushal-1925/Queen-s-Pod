The Founders
Our Story

Born from Sisterhood & A Passion for Nature

Queen's Pod was envisioned by sisters Anna and Senorita as a bridge between the nature-centric, organic architecture of Bali and the lush, majestic beauty of their home state, Sikkim.

Every bamboo framework, structural curve, and transparent dome was carefully designed to complement the gentle sounds of the Ranikhola river. We welcome you to experience eco-luxury hospitality at its peak.

The Ambience & Culture

Capture the Moment

Every corner of Queen's Pod is a tribute to eco-luxury architecture, Balinese serenity, and the vibrant culture of Sikkim.

Engineering Wonder

The Suspended Glass Deck

Our pride and joy: a state-of-the-art wooden and reinforced glass deck structure cantilevered directly above the rushing waters of the Ranikhola river. Dine while looking down at the natural stones and cascading currents, a sensory experience unlike any other in Gangtok.

Cultural Connection

Complimentary Sikkimese Attire

We celebrate our rich local heritage. Guests are invited to dress up in our collection of free, authentic Sikkimese traditional outfits (Kho/Bakhu). Our staff will gladly help you style the robes, and you can walk out onto the decks to take stunning, memorable photographs.

Organic Design

Bali-Inspired Architecture

Constructed by skilled local artisans using sustainable bamboo species, our buildings represent a unique blend of Balinese organic framework curves and modern geometric styling. The sweeping roof shapes mimic the grand arches of the Sydney Opera House, glowing bright after sunset.

Warm Welcome

Spacious & 100% Pet-Friendly

At Queen's Pod, we believe that family outings should include everyone. Our wide floor layouts, riverside gardens, and wooden decks provide plenty of room for your pet dogs and cats to stretch out. We even offer fresh water bowls and custom pet-safe treat platters.
